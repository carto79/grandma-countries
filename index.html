The Woman Behind the Map

This map is inspired by Faustina Warren Thomas, born in February 1926 in Trinidad and Tobago. In her lifetime, she witnessed her own country gain independence from Britain in 1962, and watched as more than half the nations on Earth were born. In February 2026, the Warren and Taitt families celebrated her 100th birthday — honored by the Trinidad and Tobago National Centenarian Programme on Carnival Sunday.
