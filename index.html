The Woman Behind the Map

This map is dedicated to my grandmother, who has seen the imaginary lines that divide societies and cultures be redrawn a multitude of times. Born in February of 1926 in Trinidad and Tobago, she has witnessed the rise of many new nations throughout the 20th century.

Join her on the latest entry into her journey by viewing her centennial birthday party held at the Careneage Regional Community Center, Carenage, Trinidad and Tobago.
